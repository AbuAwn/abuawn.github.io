About Me

Passionate about International After-Sales Support | Bridging Technical Expertise and Commercial Insight


Managing international after-sales support has been an incredible learning journey. Our goal is to ensure that customers maintain the same excitement and trust they felt on the first day they chose our ever-evolving products—products that present real challenges and opportunities every single day.


Being at the intersection of technical development and manufacturing on one side, and customer-facing commercial operations across various countries on the other, has given me a unique perspective. This role enables me to understand how the entire system functions and, more importantly, what products are needed in each market.


Mathematics is more than a discipline for me; it's a way of life that shapes my rational understanding of the world around me. I've been fortunate to work alongside remarkable people who have guided me along the way. While engineering once appealed to me as a way to satisfy my practical curiosity, I've always considered myself, or at least strived to be, a mathematician at heart.
